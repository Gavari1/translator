A short introduction to the Gavári language

What is Gavári?

Gavári is a constructed island language designed to feel natural, spoken, and human. It favors rhythm, clarity, and meaning over strict grammatical precision.

It is meant to be learned through usage — not through automatic translation.

How Gavári Expresses Meaning

Gavári does not rely on heavy verb conjugation. Instead, meaning is shaped by particles, word order, and context.

  • Time is marked with particles like pa (past) fa (future)and wa (conditional)
  • Intensity is expressed through compounds and repetition
  • Many ideas are built by combining simple roots
rual-poa
light rain
rual-muia
flood · heavy rain

Demonstratives & “Things”

Gavári uses distance and number to shape meaning instead of strict plurality.

ni tālo
this thing
naas tālo
those things
tālo-saru
everything
